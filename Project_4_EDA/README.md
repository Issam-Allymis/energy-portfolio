# Energy Consumption Time Series Analysis
## Overview
This project analyses time based energy consumption data to identify patterns, trends and key drivers of demand. The goal is to understand how energy usage changes over time and what factors influence peak demand.

---

## Objective 
To explore and analyse energy consumption patterns across time, focusing on:
- Seasonal trends
- Daily usage patterns
- Human behavioural drivers of demand

---

## Tools and Technlogies
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib.pyplot
- Google Colab

---

## Dataset
The Dataset contains hourly energy consumption data, including:
- Datetime
- Energy usage (MW)

---

## Key Analyses
### 1. Time Series Visualisation
- Plotted energy consumption over time to identify overall trends
  **Finding:**
  Energy usage Fluctuates significantly over time, showing clear cyclical patterns.
  
---

### 2. Seasonal Analysis
- grouped data by month to analyse seasonal trends
  **Finding:**
  Energy consumption peaks during summer month July and August, Likely due to increased demand for cooling systems.

---

### 3. Hourly Demand Analysis
- Analysed average energy usage by hour of the day
  **Finding:**
  Demand gradually begins to increase from 8am and peaks around 6-7pm, and declines overnight.

---

## Key Insights
- Energy demands is not constant and fluctuates, following a predictable pattern.
- Summer months drive the highest energy consumption.
- Peak demand occurs in the evening due to overalpping residential and commercial usage.
- Energy consumption is strongly influenced by humand activity.

---

## Business Implications
- Energy providers can prepare for peak summer demand.
- Infrastructure planning can focus on peak evening hours.
- Resource allocation can be optimised using predictable demand cycles.

---

## Conclusion
Energy consumption is highly cyclical, with clear seasonal and daily patterns. Understanding these trends allow for better forecasting, improved efficiency, and more effective management of energy resources.
